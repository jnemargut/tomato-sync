## DESCRIPTION (paste into the page body)

**Tomato Sync** is a little desktop companion for your Playdate, the way iTunes used
to sync an iPod. Plug your Playdate in, and Tomato Sync notices which jontomato apps
you have and quietly fills them with fresh stuff over USB. No menus, no fiddling.

Leave it docked overnight and wake up to new episodes, today's gift, and your latest
mixtape, already on the device. It even keeps the apps themselves up to date.

**It feeds:**

- **Crankcaster** — your podcasts. Pulls the newest unplayed episodes for every show
  in your stack, trims what you've finished.
- **Read Watch Listen Play** — the daily four-pillar gift: a story, a short film, a
  radio play, a crossword. Grabs today's, clears out yesterday's.
- **lilmixtape** — your current mixtape's tracks, ready to play offline.

**How it works**

1. Wake your Playdate and plug it in.
2. Tomato Sync detects your apps and syncs each one. Leave it docked overnight and it
   keeps pulling new stuff on a timer.
3. Hit **Eject** in the morning (always eject before unplugging) and your Playdate
   reboots with everything.

**Also:** auto-updates the Playdate apps to their latest build before syncing ·
one-click links to manage a stack or open a mixtape on the web · lives quietly in
your menu bar / system tray as a little tomato.

Free. Made by jontomato.

### Download & install

Pick the file for your computer:

**🍎 macOS — `Tomato-Sync-0.2.1-arm64.dmg`**
*Apple Silicon Macs (M1/M2/M3/M4).*
1. Open the `.dmg` and drag **Tomato Sync** into Applications.
2. First launch only: **right-click the app → Open → Open** (it's unsigned, so a
   normal double-click gets blocked the first time). After that it opens normally.
3. It lives in your **menu bar** as a little tomato.

**🪟 Windows — `Tomato-Sync-Setup-0.2.1.exe`  (ALPHA)**
*Windows 10/11. Heads up: the Windows version hasn't been tested on real hardware
yet, treat it as experimental.*
1. Run the installer. Windows SmartScreen may warn since it's unsigned, click
   **More info → Run anyway**.
2. It lives in your **system tray** as a little tomato.

**Using it:** wake your Playdate, plug it in over USB, and Tomato Sync auto-detects
your apps and syncs. Leave it docked overnight to keep pulling new stuff; hit
**Eject** when you're done (always eject before unplugging).

**You'll need:** a Playdate with one or more jontomato apps already installed
(Crankcaster, Read Watch Listen Play, lilmixtape), and macOS or Windows.
